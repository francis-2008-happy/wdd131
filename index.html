Official Flag of Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is the most populous country on the continent, with over 200 million people. It is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and significant economic influence. Bordered by Niger, Chad, Cameroon, and Benin, Nigeria has a vast landscape that includes rainforests, savannas, and the Niger River, which plays a crucial role in agriculture and trade. The country gained independence from British colonial rule on October 1, 1960 and has since developed into a major political and economic power in Africa. Nigeria operates under a federal system with 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory (Abuja) as its administrative center. Lagos, the former capital, remains the largest city and a key economic hub.
