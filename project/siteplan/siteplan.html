Tricia’s Hairline - Website Plan

Site Name

Tricia’s Hairline

This name reflects the brand’s focus on premium hair products (wigs, frontals, blends) and treatments. It’s memorable and aligns with common hair industry branding.

Optional domain: triciashairline.com (example)

Site Purpose

To provide an online showcase for Tricia’s Hairline products, including:

  • Human hair wigs, frontals, and blends
  • Hair care treatments and tutorials
  • Customer testimonials and before/after examples
  • Booking for custom orders or consultations

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. "What’s the best lace frontal for beginners?" → Will be answered in a "Beginner’s Guide" section.
  2. "How do I maintain my human hair wig?" → Addressed in a "Hair Care Tips" blog.

Color Schema

#8b5a2b (Headings)
Black (Buttons/CTAs)
#000000 (Accents)
f0d6eb (Background)

Usage: Brown (#8b5a2b) for headings, black (#000000) for buttons/CTAs, soft purple (f0d6eb) as page background.

Typography

Primary Font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for body text.

Secondary Font: 'Playfair Display' (serif) for headings.
